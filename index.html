Front-end development

Front-end development

In a struggle for exceptional UX we tend to win the hearts of your clients with modern weaponry: GRUNTs, PREPROS’, SASS and LESS. Our code is always on pair with project’s parameters and works perfectly fine on mobile and desktop platforms. The priority is aesthetics and full compatibility. Effective extras, like parallaxes and 3D roll-overs, are always an option. Fresh and clear front is a calling card of a modern company – it creates positive first impression and is a big leap towards winning user’s commitment.

Back-end development

Mobile applications

Team

Team

We are fluent in machine language and we’ve successfully communicated with them for over 5 years. Our specialty is creating reliable, personalized software for web instruments (internet tools). At Smultron we undertake different projects varying in type and complexity – from designing microsites, mobile apps and e-stores to very complex online research tools and information portals. Inhuman performance and creativity have won us trust of recognizable companies in Poland and abroad. We operate mostly as subcontractors for big and middle-size advertising and interactive agencies, but we’re always interested in intriguing projects and we constantly look for new challenges.
